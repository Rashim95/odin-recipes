Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Peanut Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, cream the butter, peanut butter and sugars until light and fluffy, 5-7 minutes
  2. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture and mix well. Stir in chips
  3. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets
  4. Bake at 350Â° for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Cool for 2 minutes before removing to wire racks
